How it works?


All you need to do is look up the pet you wish to adopt while checking its description and needs. Once you are done deciding remember to note its ID No. and send a mail to us with the ID no. of the pet, our customer handler will ask you few questions about your livelihood and verify few documents. Once the verification is cleared, you will be able to get your new family member from our nearest shelter. We hope they find a good and lovely home with you!
