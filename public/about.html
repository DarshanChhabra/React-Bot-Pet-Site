About Us






Our goal is to become the most preferred way of finding a new pet online. Every pet deserves a good life. With a vision to end animal suffering, we rescue, heal, adopt and advocate for sick, injured and abused animals. We are the only association which organizes emergency rescue operations for animals in more than 17 states of India!, Only a call away on our Emergency phone No. 1011.
